Frequently Asked Questions

What hours are you available?

I'm generally available Monday through Friday from 9am to 6pm East Africa Time (EAT). However, I can be flexible to accommodate different time zones and urgent requests.

How do you handle confidential information?

I take confidentiality very seriously. I sign NDAs when required and follow strict security protocols to ensure your information remains private and secure. I never share client information with third parties without explicit permission.

What is your turnaround time for tasks?

Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the task and your package level. For standard tasks, I typically respond within 24 hours for Basic, 12 hours for Standard, and 6 hours for Premium packages. For urgent tasks, please let me know, and I'll prioritize accordingly.

How do we communicate?

I'm flexible with communication methods and can adapt to your preferences. I commonly use email, Slack, WhatsApp, Zoom, or any other platform you prefer. We'll establish a primary and backup communication method during onboarding to ensure we can always stay in touch."

What if I need to cancel or pause services?

I understand that business needs can change. I require a 14-day notice for cancellation. For pausing services, please let me know as soon as possible, and we can discuss arrangements. Unused hours from monthly packages can be carried over for up to 30 days.

How do you track hours worked?

I use time-tracking software to accurately log all work performed. I provide detailed reports showing tasks completed and time spent. This ensures transparency and helps you understand how your hours are being utilized.
